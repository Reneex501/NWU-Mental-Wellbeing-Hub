Welcome

Your safe space for mental health support at NWU.

The NWU Mental Wellbeing Hub is a student-centred digital platform designed to support the mental health and emotional wellbeing of students at North-West University. The website serves as an accessible, user-friendly space where students can find reliable information, practical self-help tools, and guidance on where to seek support within the university environment.

The platform is specifically tailored to address common challenges faced by students, such as academic stress, anxiety, adjustment to university life, and social pressures. By combining informative content with interactive features, the website moves beyond simply providing information and instead actively engages users in managing their wellbeing.
